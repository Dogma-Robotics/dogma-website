01 — Anatomy

From bone scan
to hardware.

3D scan · Human hand skeleton · Printed prototype
27
Degrees of freedom — hand
Every proximal, medial, and distal phalanx modeled at true scale from a 3D scan of a human hand. Printed in rigid polymer for structural prototyping before final material selection.
21
Ligaments per finger
Each finger replicates the exact count and geometry of human ligament structures. 21 ligaments per digit provide joint stability, passive force transmission, and the compliant behavior that makes human grasp robust to perturbation.
3
Wrist DOF
Spherical wrist joint with central cable channel for tendon routing. Full flexion/extension, radial/ulnar deviation, and pronation/supination within human anatomical range.

05 — Control

Nine layers.
One emergent system.

From 1 kHz safety supervision to 0.5 Hz language reasoning. Each layer operates at its natural timescale, with hard overrides flowing downward through M0.

M0
1-5 kHz
Safety Supervisor
Hard override on all commands. Pressure, force, slew-rate clamps. Leak/jam detection. E-stop and safe-open.
M1
500-1000 Hz
Pressure Servo
PID + anti-windup per McKibben muscle. Decouples hydraulic dynamics from force control.
M2
200-500 Hz
Force / Impedance Control
Primary control layer. Regulates tendon tension |F|. Antagonistic preload F0 tunes stiffness without net torque.
M3
100-300 Hz
Proprioception Estimator
Inverts McKibben model p,|F| to strain. Solves regularized least-squares for theta-hat via J_L Jacobian.
M4
30 fps
Vision & Object Tracking
Stereo RGB producing SE(3) pose for up to O objects. Confidence score g_cam gates fusion in M5.
M5
60-120 Hz
Fusion Observer / GRU
Fuses vision + proprioception + tactile. Internal spacetime w-hat as GRU latent. SE(3) tracking with Lie group updates.
M6
100-300 Hz
Reflex Layer
Anti-slip and anti-crush reflexes via sparse contact matrix W_{i,z}. Independent of policy. Operates under visual occlusion.
M7
10-30 Hz
Policy / Task Layer
Outputs (F0, dF_M7, K*, D*). Active Inference with epistemic probing and quantum-like mode selection.
M8
0.5-5 Hz
Language & Skill Interface
LLM/VLA generator + feasibility critic (SayCan-style). Natural language to skill sequences. All outputs validated by M0.
Core equation — tendon jacobian
tau_j = -Sum_i F_i * dL_i(theta)/dtheta_j + tau_pass(j)

J_L(theta) in R^(N x J), N=54, J=30
Torque emerges from routing geometry, not angle commands. theta(t) is never measured — estimated from pressure and force via the McKibben inversion model.
Force reference — M2
F*(t) = clip(F0(t) + dF_M7 + dF_M6, 0, F_max)
F0 is antagonistic preload: baseline tension tuning stiffness without net torque. Computed via QP: argmin ||J_L^T F||^2 at 10-30 Hz.
State fusion — M5
theta_hat = g_cam * theta_cam + (1-g_cam) * theta_prop

w_hat_t = GRU(w_hat_{t-1}, y_t)
SE(3) object pose updated via Lie group exponential map. GRU hidden state w_hat is the internal spacetime representation maintained across the control cycle.
